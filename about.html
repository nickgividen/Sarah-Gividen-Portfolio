About

Sarah Gividen resides in Houston, Texas with her husband Nick and their daughter Cora, son Paul, and dog JT (named after James Taylor.) Along with her solo work as a singer/ songwriter, Sarah and Nick make up the band Most Vivid, lead worship together, and write songs about their experience of being loved by Jesus Christ. Her favorite hobbies are reading books, binging true crime documentaries, enjoying all things Tex Mex, and working out to justify eating so much Tex Mex.
